Welcome, hi, hello! My name is James Stemple and I'm a video editor, documentarian, and animator. I love anything visually stimulating and dabble with technology that aids me in my quest to push myself creativley.

I fell in love with animation at a young age—all too often my mother would catch me staying up late trying to watch Adult Swim—that led me to exploring video editing and animation software later in life. I'm a quick learner but overcoming technical challenges is always a part of the process (and I love that part as well!). Putting together a video editing or animation project is a lot like a puzzle to me.

I'm skilled at using many of the Adobe Creative Cloud apps, such as premiere, after effects, photoshop, and I've dabbled with illustrator. I've used blender and maya for 3D animations (I'm a big fan of rigging). I know some basic python as well as HTML and CSS (which has brought this website up for your viewing pleasure).

Years ago while watching an animated short, he said to himself "yeah, I wanna do that," and so he did.
He's skilled in the Adobe Creative Cloud suite as well as other programs such as Maya for 3D animation
and avidemux for datamoshing (man does he love datamoshing).

He's driven by the need for self-expression and passion for manipulating pixels. Some would say James is determined,
sometimes stubbornly so, like when he decided to make his own website from scratch instead of getting a premade one.
